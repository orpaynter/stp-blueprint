Technical Risks

HIGH Integration Complexity

Challenge: Multiple services, databases, and components need seamless integration.

Impact: Delays in launch, potential system instability.

Mitigation: Phased integration, comprehensive testing, dedicated DevOps.

MEDIUM Scalability Under Load

Challenge: Platform performance with rapid user growth.

Impact: Poor user experience, system outages.

Mitigation: Load testing, auto-scaling infrastructure, performance monitoring.

MEDIUM AI Model Accuracy

Challenge: Damage detection models may have false positives/negatives.

Impact: User trust issues, insurance claim disputes.

Mitigation: Continuous training, human validation, confidence scoring.

Business Risks

HIGH Market Adoption Speed

Challenge: Traditional industry slow to adopt new technology.

Impact: Extended runway to profitability.

Mitigation: Strategic partnerships, pilot programs, gradual rollout.

MEDIUM Competitive Response

Challenge: Large players may develop competing solutions.

Impact: Market share erosion, pricing pressure.

Mitigation: First-mover advantage, continuous innovation, patent protection.

LOW Regulatory Changes

Challenge: Insurance or construction industry regulations.

Impact: Compliance costs, feature modifications.

Mitigation: Regulatory monitoring, flexible architecture, industry partnerships.
