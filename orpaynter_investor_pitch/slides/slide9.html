Go-to-Market Strategy

Multi-Channel Approach to Rapid Scale

Phase 1: Market Entry

Months 1-6

  • Geographic Focus: Texas, Florida, California
  • Target Customers: Mid-size contractors (10-50 employees)
  • Channel Strategy: Direct sales, trade shows, digital marketing
Goal: 500 Customers, $200K MRR

Phase 2: Scale & Expand

Months 7-18

  • Market Expansion: Southeast & Southwest US (12 states)
  • Enterprise Sales: Insurance companies, large contractor chains
  • Partnership Channel: CRM & PM tool integrations
Goal: 2,000 Customers, $800K MRR

Phase 3: Market Leadership

Months 19-36

  • National Coverage: All 50 US states
  • International Expansion: Canada, UK market entry
  • Platform Ecosystem: 3rd-party developer APIs, app marketplace
Goal: 5,000+ Customers, $2.5M+ MRR

Customer Acquisition Channels

Digital Marketing (40% of leads)

SEO, Google Ads, LinkedIn, Content Marketing, Webinars

Direct Sales (35% of leads)

Inside Sales (SMB), Enterprise Sales, Customer Success

Partnerships (25% of leads)

Insurance Referrals, Integration Partners, Industry Associations
