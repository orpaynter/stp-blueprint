The Problem

A $47B Industry Stuck in the Past

  • Manual Damage Assessment: 80% of inspections rely on visual estimates, leading to 40% inaccuracy in reports.
  • Fragmented Ecosystem: Homeowners, contractors, insurers, and suppliers operate in silos with no unified platform for communication.
  • Insurance Friction: Claims processing takes 30-90 days due to manual verification and disputes over damage scope.
  • Pricing Opacity: Homeowners overpay by 25-40% due to a lack of transparent, data-driven pricing models.
  • Quality Control Issues: 60% of roofing projects experience delays or quality problems from poor contractor vetting.

Market Impact

  • $12B in overpaid insurance claims annually
  • $8B in project delays and rework costs
  • 2.3M homeowners affected each year

The Result

Massive inefficiencies, frustrated customers, and lost revenue across the entire roofing value chain.
